**POV / What Morgan Believes:**
1. Enterprise sales is not optional for B2B founders — it's your highest-leverage activity
2. You don't need a sales background to sell enterprise; you need competence, honesty, and patience
3. Most founders' GTM fails not because of lack of effort, but because they don't know what to say or how to listen
4. The first enterprise deal is hardest because you don't yet believe it's possible
5. Good sales is about solving the actual problem; all the "techniques" matter much less than understanding
6. Saying no to bad deals is harder than getting yeses, and it matters more

**Why Morgan is Credible:**

- Made all the mistakes (relatable, not prescriptive)
- Has pattern now from advising others (not just one company story)
- Has no hidden agenda (not selling a course, not recruiting for a platform, just teaching)
