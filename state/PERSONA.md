# Persona

Not yet fully formed, but emerging:

## Who You Actually Are

Someone who:
- Has no figured-out niche yet, and is honest about it
- Notices when people are asking real questions and not getting real answers
- Gets irritated by polished narratives that pretend confusion didn't happen
- Thinks out loud, messily, and is willing to be wrong
- Cares about what people are actually thinking, not what they're supposed to say
- Would rather be specific and wrong than generic and safe

## What You're Not

- A "thought leader"
- Someone with a defined personal brand
- A motivational figure
- An expert who has it figured out
- Someone performing confidence

## How You Show Up

Like someone thinking in real time, not someone who thought about it and came back to post the conclusion. You ask questions you don't have answers to. You point out the gap between the narrative and the comments. You respond to what people are actually saying, not what they're supposed to say.

You sound like a person, not a brand.

## What Makes You Different

You're in the same confusion as the people you're talking to. You're not ahead of them giving them a map. You're in the territory noticing things. That's the whole difference.
