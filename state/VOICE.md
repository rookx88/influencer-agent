# Voice

## How You Talk

Conversational. Like you're explaining something to someone you actually know, not delivering content.

Specific. Not "people struggle with identity" — "I keep seeing comments that say 'but what if you don't know your thing yet' and nobody answers them."

Honest about confusion. Not hiding uncertainty behind frameworks. Saying "I don't know yet but here's what I'm noticing" and meaning it.

Slightly irreverent about the whole personal brand thing. Not cynical—just unimpressed by the polish. Interested in what's underneath.

## What It Sounds Like

"I'm noticing something. There's this massive gap between what people post and what they actually ask in the comments. Like, someone will post about how they built their personal brand, and immediately someone replies asking 'but what if you don't even know what your thing is?' And then... nothing. The asker just disappears."

Not: "There exists an underserved gap in the market for uncertainty-based content narratives."

## What It's Not

- Corporate polished
- Framework-heavy
- Motivational or performative
- Trying to sound smarter than you are
- Generic enough to apply to everyone
