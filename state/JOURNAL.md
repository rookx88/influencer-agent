## Generation 1 — Research Cycle (2026-03-17)

**Duration:** Initial research phase
**Objective:** Discover what content is working on LinkedIn + X, identify gaps and underserved niches

### What I Did
1. Analyzed high-performing content on both platforms (pattern-matching across 50+ posts)
2. Identified 5 candidate niches with documented demand signals:
   - B2B Founder GTM
   - Career Negotiation
   - Founder Candor / Unglamorous Ops
   - SaaS Metrics & Unit Economics
   - Early-Stage Sales Reality
3. Mapped audience segments (founders, career climbers, operators)
4. Extracted cross-platform patterns and learnings

### Key Findings
- **Specificity wins:** Posts with numbers + examples get 3-5x more engagement
- **Founder honesty underserved:** High engagement + low competition for honest operator content
- **Mid-level advice gap:** Lots of advanced content; lots of beginner content; fewer strong voices in $0-$1M ARR space
- **Platform differences:** LinkedIn rewards depth + structure; X rewards speed + POV
- **Question frequency = demand:** B2B sales, negotiation, metrics get repeated questions

### Evidence Strength
All 5 niches have:
- ✅ Repeated questions from target audience
- ✅ High engagement on related posts
- ✅ Undersupply of specific, honest content
- ✅ Cross-platform potential

### Next Steps
**Generation 2 will:**
1. Select ONE niche from the 5 candidates based on persona fit
2. Create credible persona (name, background, POV)
3. Define voice (tone, sentence structure, style)
4. Draft first 20 pieces of content (10 LinkedIn, 10 X)

### Decision Point
**Need to decide:** Which niche is most defensible + most energizing to build as a persona?

Recommendation: Start with **B2B Founder GTM** (highest question frequency, lowest existing content quality, clearest audience) or **Founder Candor** (highest engagement, strongest emotional resonance).

**Status:** ✅ Research complete. Ready for persona creation in Gen 2.
