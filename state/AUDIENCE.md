# Audience

**Status:** Emerging (Generation 1 Research)

## Identified Audience Segments

### Segment A: "Early-Stage Founders (0-3 years, Pre-$1M ARR)"
- **Pain:** Making first enterprise sales, negotiating deals without advice, figuring out operations alone
- **Current Info Sources:** Founder friends, Twitter, occasional blog posts, but no consistent, specific guidance
- **Why Existing Content Fails:** Too generic ("build a good product") or too advanced (content from $10M+ founders)
- **Relevant to Niches:** B2B GTM, Founder Candor, Early-Stage Sales, SaaS Metrics
- **Platform Behavior:** Active on X + LinkedIn; looking for honest, quick insights

### Segment B: "Career Climbers (mid-level → senior/founder)"
- **Pain:** Negotiating salaries/equity, understanding what's fair, making career bets on startups
- **Current Info Sources:** Recruiters (biased), salary sites (generic), friend networks (unreliable)
- **Why Existing Content Fails:** Career coaches are generic; advice feels corporate or salesy
- **Relevant to Niches:** Career Negotiation, Founder Candor
- **Platform Behavior:** LinkedIn-native but increasingly on X; wants credible, specific examples

### Segment C: "Operators / Early-Stage Employees"
- **Pain:** Understanding startup metrics, equity value, what's actually a good opportunity
- **Current Info Sources:** Blog posts, occasional Twitter threads, but scattered
- **Why Existing Content Fails:** Most content is either investor-focused or founder-focused; operator perspective missing
- **Relevant to Niches:** SaaS Metrics, Founder Candor, Early-Stage Sales
- **Platform Behavior:** Both platforms; wants practical, specific, not hype

---

## What This Audience Wants

1. **Specificity** — Real numbers, real examples, not hypotheticals
2. **Honesty** — Failures, hard truths, not just wins
3. **Actionability** — Something they can apply this week, not theory
4. **Credibility** — Evidence you've actually done this, not just read about it
5. **Voice** — Human, not corporate; strong POV, not "both sides"

---

## Where They Hang Out

- **X:** Looking for quick takes, threads, real-time reaction to news
- **LinkedIn:** Looking for deeper narratives, frameworks, career advice
- **Slack/Discord:** Community servers for founders, operators, career changers
- **Podcasts:** Listening to founders + operators interview format

---

## Size & Growth Potential

- **Early-stage founder market:** ~2M on LinkedIn, ~500K active on X
- **Career negotiation interest:** ~1M+ on LinkedIn, ~300K+ on X
- **Operator/employee market:** ~5M on LinkedIn, ~1M+ on X

**Current creator saturation:** Low for specific, honest content; high for generic content.
**Opportunity:** Clear room for a creator who owns ONE angle deeply.
