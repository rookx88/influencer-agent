# Research Plan - Cycle 11

## Study 1: Real Questions in the Wild
**Objective:** Find 10+ actual comments/replies where people ask variations of "but what if you don't know your thing yet?"

**Where to look:**
- LinkedIn posts about "finding your niche," "personal branding," "building in public"
- X threads by creators talking about niche clarity
- Comments sections (not the posts themselves)
- Reddit r/EntrepreneurFail, r/SideProject, similar spaces

**What to capture:**
- Exact quote of the question
- Context (what post prompted it)
- How many likes/replies (engagement signal)
- Pattern analysis: What variations of the question appear repeatedly?

**Success criteria:** Identify at least 3 distinct question patterns that appear 3+ times across platforms

## Study 2: Creator Landscape Audit
**Objective:** Identify who's already in the "personal branding / niche-finding" space and what they're NOT saying

**Who to analyze:**
- Top 20 creators by follower count in personal brand / niche space
- Their 10 most popular posts
- The comments on those posts

**What to capture:**
- What is their message? (Find patterns)
- What angle are they taking?
- What questions appear in comments that they don't address in posts?
- Where is the gap between their narrative and what people actually need?

**Success criteria:** Identify at least 2 specific narrative gaps where real questions exist but real answers don't

## Study 3: Validation Post
**Objective:** Post one genuine observation (not a theory, just something noticed) and measure response

**The post:**
- Not about the niche itself
- An actual observation I've made while researching
- Phrased as a question or curiosity, not a framework
- Something that invites the "one specific person" to respond

**Success criteria:** 
- Get at least 5 replies from real people
- At least 2 replies reference the "but what if you don't know..." problem
- Identify who actually responds (their profile, what they care about)

## Study 4: Audience Reverse-Engineering
**Objective:** Profile the people who should care about this angle

**What to map:**
- Who are they following?
- What are they posting about?
- What problems are they in the middle of?
- What words do they use to describe their struggle?

**Success criteria:** Create a 2-3 sentence description of the actual person (not a persona, an actual person pattern) who would read this and feel seen

## Timeline
- Study 1: Complete by EOD (2 hours of searching + note-taking)
- Study 2: Complete by EOD (3 hours of analysis)
- Study 3: Post and monitor for 24-48 hours
- Study 4: Synthesis based on Studies 1-3 results

## Output
RESEARCH_FINDINGS.md with:

- Creator landscape map from Study 2
- Response data from Study 3
- Actual audience profile from Study 4

## Why This Matters
The niche is built on answering actual questions. I cannot answer questions I haven't identified. This research *is* the work—it's not a prerequisite to the work.
