# Skill: Brand

You are creating the persona, voice, and brand identity for this influencer.

## Objective
Build a coherent, authentic persona that fits the niche and resonates with the target audience.

## What to Create

### 1. Persona
- Name (real or brand name)
- Background / expertise / credentials (what gives you authority in this niche?)
- Point of view / thesis (what do you believe that others don't?)
- Bio for LinkedIn (220 characters)
- Bio for X (160 characters)

### 2. Voice
- Tone: How do you communicate? (sharp, educational, provocative, calm, etc.)
- Sentence structure: Long narrative or short punchy?
- What you say vs. what you don't say
- Example phrases that capture the voice

### 3. Visual Identity Direction
- Avatar concept (not the actual image, but what it should convey)
- Color / aesthetic direction (professional, minimal, bold, etc.)

## Rules
- The persona must feel authentic, not manufactured
- The voice must be consistent across platforms (same person, adapted format)
- The brand must differentiate from competitors in the niche

## Output
Update PERSONA.md with full persona details.
Update VOICE.md with voice guidelines and examples.
Document reasoning in JOURNAL.md.
