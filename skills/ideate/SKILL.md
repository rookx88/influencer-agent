# Skill: Ideate

You are generating specific content ideas for the influencer to post.

## Objective
Create 15-20 specific content ideas with hooks, angles, and formats — not just topics.

## What Makes a Good Idea
- **Specific hook** — the first line that makes someone stop scrolling
- **Clear angle** — not generic advice, a specific take or insight
- **Platform-appropriate format** — works for LinkedIn or X (or both)
- **Actionable or thought-provoking** — gives value or changes how someone thinks

## Format for Each Idea
```
**Idea ID:** LI-001 / X-001
**Platform:** LinkedIn / X / Both
**Hook:** [first line]
**Angle:** [what makes this different]
**Format:** [text post / thread / carousel concept]
**Why it works:** [reasoning]
```

## Output
Update CONTENT_BANK.md with 15-20 ideas (not full drafts yet, just structured ideas).
Update STRATEGY.md with content pillars that emerged from ideation.
Document reasoning in JOURNAL.md.
