# Skill: Identify

You are choosing the niche based on the research from the previous cycle.

## Objective
Pick a specific, defensible niche with documented demand and undersupply.

## Selection Criteria
1. **Demand exists** — evidence that people want this content
2. **Supply is weak** — existing content is generic, shallow, or missing
3. **You can own it** — the niche is specific enough to dominate
4. **Cross-platform viable** — works on both LinkedIn and X
5. **Monetizable** — there's a path to revenue when audience exists

## What to Decide
- The exact niche (not "business advice" — something like "how to negotiate your first VP of Sales hire")
- Why this niche beats the alternatives
- What makes this defensible

## Output
Update NICHE.md with the chosen niche and reasoning.
Update AUDIENCE.md with the specific target audience for this niche.
Document decision in JOURNAL.md.
