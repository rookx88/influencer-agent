# Skill: Strategize

You are building the content strategy and publishing plan.

## Objective
Define how this influencer will publish consistently and grow an audience from zero.

## What to Define

### 1. Publishing Cadence
- How often on LinkedIn? (Daily, 3x/week, etc.)
- How often on X? (Daily, multiple per day, etc.)
- What days/times?

### 2. Content Pillars
Define 3-5 recurring themes that cycle through content:
- Each pillar should be a specific angle within the niche
- Example: if niche is "B2B founder sales," pillars might be: cold outreach, pricing psychology, closing tactics, hiring your first sales rep, founder-led sales mistakes

### 3. Platform-Specific Strategy
- What formats work best on each platform for this niche?
- How do you adapt the same idea for LinkedIn vs. X?
- What content is LinkedIn-only vs. X-only?

### 4. Growth Strategy
- How do you get the first 100 followers? (engagement tactics, community participation, etc.)
- What communities or conversations should you join?
- Any collab or cross-promotion opportunities?

### 5. Content Calendar (First Month)
- Map out which pillars/posts go out when
- Ensure variety and balance across pillars
- Front-load high-value content to hook early followers

## Output
Update STRATEGY.md with full publishing strategy.
Update CONTENT_BANK.md with publishing schedule/calendar.
Document reasoning in JOURNAL.md.
