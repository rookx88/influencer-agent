# Rubric: content-quality

**What it measures:** Is the drafted content actually good? Would it perform?

## Scoring Guide
| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 0.0–0.3 | No content drafted or content is generic/weak |
| 0.3–0.6 | Content exists but hooks are weak, value is unclear |
| 0.6–0.8 | Good content with strong hooks and clear value |
| 0.8–0.95 | Excellent content that's ready to post immediately |
| 0.95–1.0 | Standout content that's better than competitors in the niche |

## Key Questions
- Do the hooks stop the scroll?
- Is there clear value (teaches something or shifts perspective)?
- Is it ready to copy-paste and post immediately?
