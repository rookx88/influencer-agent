# Rubric: niche-clarity

**What it measures:** Is the niche specific, defensible, and backed by research evidence?

## Scoring Guide
| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 0.0–0.3 | No niche identified or niche is generic ("business advice," "tech content") |
| 0.3–0.6 | Niche identified but not specific enough or no demand evidence |
| 0.6–0.8 | Specific niche with some evidence of demand |
| 0.8–0.95 | Highly specific niche with documented demand + undersupply evidence |
| 0.95–1.0 | Defensible, specific, evidence-backed niche that can be owned |

## Key Questions
- Can you describe the niche in one specific sentence?
- Is there documented evidence that people want this content?
- Is the supply weak or missing?
